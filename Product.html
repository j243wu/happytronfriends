About our product

With the lack of driver assistance technology on motorcycles, motorcyclists have to take their attention off the road in front of them to get key information. Examples of this include turning around to look behind to view surroundings, focusing on a dashboard that is hard to see due to reflections from the sun and using external equipment like a GPS. All of these lower the chance of reacting to dangers ahead and increase the chances of an accident.

Given the stated problem, there is a need to provide a motorcyclist easily visible information so that they can keep their attention on the road. This would reduce the need for riders to take their attention off the road which is the problem that has been identified.
