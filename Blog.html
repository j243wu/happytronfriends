Blog

The blog page is updated on week-to-week basis

MAR 17, 2018

Minor Hiccups Before The Symposium

With the symposium coming up in less than a week, the product should be finished with minor visual flaws being worked out. However, one of the Raspberry Pi Zero boards that powers and sends data to the screen got short circuited and has fried. Efforts are being made to swap out the board for a new one to continue testing. All other components are complete and new enclosures are being created for the symposium.

MAR 10, 2018

Equipment for Symposium Acquired

Simulator is completed and helmet stand and webcam have been acquired to demo the product during the symposium. 3D models of the final enclosures have been created and are to be printed. Once the models have been printed, demo videos can be created to aid in the symposium and the poster board will be finalized.

MAR 3, 2018

Second Stages of Tests

All the individual components of the product have been completed. Tests need to be done for the overall product need to be done. The simulation software is being worked on to allow a demo of the product and demo equipment need to built/acquired. Final versions of all the housings and enclosures are to be printed for better visual appeal.

FEB 24, 2018

Reading Week Break

Reading week break, no updates this week

FEB 17, 2018

Motorcycle OBD Unit

New housing model for the helmet components have been printed. Bluetooth interfacing between the Android mobile app and the helmet components have been tested. However, the IOS app has been dropped due to limited access to a IOS mobile device. Work on the motorcycle OBD unit has begun and Bluetooth data transfer will be tested.

FEB 10, 2018

New Prism for Projection

The new prism set-up has been constructed and tests with the new projection set-up has begun. Due to the new projection set-up, a new housing for some of the components needs to be created and printed. The Android and IOS app have Google Maps API integrated but require a UI before it is available to be used on a real mobile device.

FEB 3, 2018

Preliminary Testing Started

The LED screen has been set-up and optics tests have begun. The collimating lens is not clear enough since it was printed but due to the small size of the projection set-up, a collimating lens cannot be purchased. A new prism set-up that does not require a collimating lens has been proposed.

JAN 27, 2018

3D Printing and Construction

3D housing units have been printed and hardware components are being mounted inside the housing. Construction of the product is complete for the preliminary stage. The collimating lens has also been printed and tests for the optics will being once the led screen is outputting data and images. App development for the IOS and Android app has begun.

JAN 20, 2018

All Parts Are Here

Progress for the product is on track. All the ordered parts have arrived and 3D models for the housing prototypes have been created. Electrical components will be soldered as needed and the models will be 3D printed for initial testing stages.

JAN 18, 2018

Website launched!

We lauched our frist iteration of project website today!
